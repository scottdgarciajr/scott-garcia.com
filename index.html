Case 01 · Forte Mental Fitness

A 6-agent ecosystem that turned 1,000 cold contacts into a $377K pipeline.

Jan — Apr 2026

Built a production-grade multi-agent AI system that automated customer acquisition, qualification, and support — operating across 1,000+ CRM contacts, 24 stalled MQL deals, and $377K+ in pipeline opportunities.

Approach

I built a unified agent architecture combining a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) framework with a BANT-based qualification engine. The RAG layer grounds every output in structured company knowledge — marketing, onboarding, and support stay accurate and hallucination-resistant.

The BANT layer (budget, authority, need, timeline) evaluates leads and routes them dynamically: human-in-the-loop where it matters, automated handoff where it doesn't.

Reliability

Production AI is only useful when you can trust it. I built an adversarial & stress testing harness paired with an observability pipeline:

  • 225+ adversarial prompts, 125+ simulated customer interactions
  • Structured failure logging with severity classification
  • Latency tracking and budget alerts per agent
  • Human-escalation triggers tuned per agent and lead tier

Case 03 · HackPSU Spring 2024 · ROA

14 million datapoints, 24 hours, first place out of forty-two.

March 2024
24-hour build · Penn State

ROA — Road Operational Assistant — is a computer-vision web app that brings self-driving-grade safety features to ordinary drivers. We won Best Overall at HackPSU Spring 2024.

14M
Datapoints in training set
1/42
Best Overall, HackPSU

Build time, end-to-end

My role

I owned the computer vision and ML backend — training a binary classification model on thousands of images of safe vs. unsafe road conditions, and building an algorithm that focuses on the road itself to balance model unpredictability with structured analysis.

What it does

Combines historical accident data, traffic density, and weather with live road imagery to warn drivers of risks ahead of time and prescribe driving adjustments — the safety-system layer of self-driving cars, retrofitted for everyone else.
